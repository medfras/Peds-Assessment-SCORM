# Pediatric Stages of Development

## Infant (0-1)

![Infant toy box](/static/img/minigames/sorting/toybox-blue.png)

- Rolls and sits
- Babbling
- Strong caregiver attachment

## Toddler (1-3)

![Toddler toy box](/static/img/minigames/sorting/toybox-yellow.png)

- 2-word phrases
- Parallel play
- Runs and climbs

## Preschool (3-6)

![Preschool toy box](/static/img/minigames/sorting/toybox-orange.png)

- Cooperative play
- Longer sentences
- Hops on one foot
- Draws basic shapes

## School Age (6-12)

![School age toy box](/static/img/minigames/sorting/toybox-red.png)

- Peer rules matter
- Reads and reasons
- Independent skills
